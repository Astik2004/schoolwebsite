RULES OF PKBD International Inter College

General Rules

  • Students are expected to come to the college in neat, clean and simple clothes.
  • Students should not bring discredit to the college or themselves. Insubordination and rude behavior inside or outside the college will be severely dealt with.
  • Strict silence should be maintained during class hours in the Classrooms, Library, Laboratory, Examination halls, and College premises.
  • Students should not enter the office room, staff room, or computer lab without permission.
  • Monthly tests and model examinations are part of the curriculum, and students should be sincere in taking these tests without indulging in malpractice.
  • Attend all classes on time.
  • Smoking and consumption of alcohol or drugs are strictly prohibited on campus.
  • Students must dispose of litter in designated bins to maintain campus cleanliness.
  • Bicycles, scooters, and other vehicles must be parked only in the designated parking areas.
  • Use of electronic gadgets like mobile phones, tablets, and laptops is allowed only for educational purposes and in designated areas.
  • Cheating or plagiarism in any form of academic work is strictly prohibited.

Library Rules

  • Members entering the Library must carry ID cards.
  • Books are issued on Borrower‚Äôs ID card only.
  • Books are issued Monday ‚Äì Friday 09.30 A.M. to 4.30 P.M. and on Saturday 9.30 A.M. to 1.00 P.M. on all working days.
  • The Temporary Library card is issued to those who have not yet received a Permanent ID Card from the office, which is NOT TRANSFERABLE. Sub-lending of books is a misuse of this privilege and is to be avoided.
  • Special care should always be taken that the library card is not misplaced or lost. The loss, if any, is to be reported immediately to the librarian.
  • Duplicate Library card will be issued at the discretion of the Librarian and on payment of Rs. 25/- only.
  • Do not earmark the paper of pages of a book.
  • The library is a complete SILENCE ZONE.

Dress Code Rules

  • Rule 1: Follow the prescribed uniform.
  • Rule 2: No casual clothing allowed.
  • Rule 3: Wear your ID card at all times.

College Timing Rules

  • Rule 1: College starts at 8:00 AM and ends at 3:00 PM.
  • Rule 2: Be punctual for all classes.
  • Rule 3: No loitering during class hours.

Lab Rules

  • Silence should be maintained inside the computer lab.
  • Playing games and chatting are prohibited.
  • Students should work only on their assigned systems and are responsible for the given system.
  • Chewable items are prohibited inside the lab.
  • If a student damages the system (software or hardware), the expense will be collected from him/her.
  • Students should get permission while entering & leaving the lab.
  • Chairs should be arranged properly before leaving the lab.

Parent/Guardian Rules

  • Parents are reminded that they are not absolved of their responsibility in the education of their children once their daughters/sons are admitted to the college.
  • They should follow the students‚Äô progress in studies and her/his general conduct in and outside the college.
  • Their cooperation is solicited especially to ensure regular attendance of classes.
  • Parents/Guardians must inform the college in case of any changes in contact details or address to ensure effective communication.
  • Parents/Guardians are requested to monitor their ward's use of technology and internet at home to ensure it is used appropriately and safely.
  • Parents/Guardians should ensure that their ward completes all homework and assignments on time and provide a conducive environment for studying at home.
  • Attend Parent-Teacher Meetings: Parents/Guardians are encouraged to attend parent-teacher meetings to discuss their ward's progress and address any concerns.
  • Parents are specially requested to contact the college authorities periodically to get first-hand information about the attendance, academic progress, conduct, etc., of their wards.
