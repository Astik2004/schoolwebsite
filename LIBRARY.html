📚📚Library Rules📚📚

  • Members entering the Library must carry ID cards.
  • Books are issued on Borrower’s ID card only.
  • Books are issued Monday – Friday 09.30 A.M. to 4.30 P.M. and on Saturday 9.30 A.M. to 1.00 P.M. on all working days.
  • The Temporary Library card is issued to those who have not yet received a Permanent ID Card from the office, which is NOT TRANSFERABLE. Sub-lending of books is a misuse of this privilege and is to be avoided.
  • Special care should always be taken that the library card is not misplaced or lost. The loss, if any, is to be reported immediately to the librarian.
  • Duplicate Library card will be issued at the discretion of the Librarian and on payment of Rs. 25/- only.
  • Do not earmark the paper of pages of a book.
  • The library is a complete SILENCE ZONE.

Parent/Guardian Rules

  • Parents are reminded that they are not absolved of their responsibility in the education of their children once their daughters/sons are admitted to the college.
  • They should follow the students’ progress in studies and her/his general conduct in and outside the college.
  • Their cooperation is solicited especially to ensure regular attendance of classes.
  • Parents/Guardians must inform the college in case of any changes in contact details or address to ensure effective communication.
  • Parents/Guardians are requested to monitor their ward's use of technology and internet at home to ensure it is used appropriately and safely.
  • Parents/Guardians should ensure that their ward completes all homework and assignments on time and provide a conducive environment for studying at home.
  • Attend Parent-Teacher Meetings: Parents/Guardians are encouraged to attend parent-teacher meetings to discuss their ward's progress and address any concerns.
  • Parents are specially requested to contact the college authorities periodically to get first-hand information about the attendance, academic progress, conduct, etc., of their wards.
